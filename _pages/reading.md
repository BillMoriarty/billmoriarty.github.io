# Recently Read

### Hyperion and Hyperion 2
- Wow, these were a great sci-fi pair. I did not like the cliffhanger end of Hyperion 1 but Hyperion 2 picked right up. [Link](https://www.amazon.com/Hyperion-Cantos-Book-1-ebook/dp/B004G60EHS/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&keywords=hyperion+1+and+2&qid=1589995107&s=digital-text&sr=1-1){:target="_blank"}

### Digital Minimalism
- This is the newer book from Cal Newport. I read his previous two books several times, and this is a continuation of the work-focused ideas in those books but written for your home life. It's worth reading. [Link](https://www.amazon.com/Digital-Minimalism-Choosing-Focused-Noisy-ebook/dp/B07DBRBP7G/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&keywords=Digital+Minimalism&qid=1589995159&s=digital-text&sr=1-1){:target="_blank"}

### In Love with the World: A Monk's Journey Through the Bardos of Living and Dying
- !!! I recommend this book to many friends. A few years ago, a reincarnated Buddhist monk who was never let alone by senior monks secretly plans to disappear and become a wandering monk for 3 years. He sneaks out in the middle of night, travels, nearly dies, and lucidly writes what he learned. [Link](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07GD2N7M3/ref=x_gr_w_glide_sin?caller=Goodreads&callerLink=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F41429805-in-love-with-the-world&tag=x_gr_w_glide_sin-20){:target="_blank"}

### Coding Games in Scratch: A Step-By-Step Visual Guide to Building Your Own Computer Games
- I bought this to teach my daughter coding. It's the best book I've found for her at age 8. The illustrations are superb and delightful. Get the paperback, not the kindle. [Link](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1465477330/ref=x_gr_w_bb_glide_sin?ie=UTF8&tag=x_gr_w_bb_glide_sin-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=1465477330&SubscriptionId=1MGPYB6YW3HWK55XCGG2){:target="_blank"}

### How Not to Kill Your Houseplant: Survival Tips for the Horticulturally Challenged
- 2019: the first year I had houseplants survive the full year! This book works. You'll want this in hardcover as the color illustrations are lovely. [Link](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1465463305/ref=x_gr_w_bb_glide_sin?ie=UTF8&tag=x_gr_w_bb_glide_sin-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=1465463305&SubscriptionId=1MGPYB6YW3HWK55XCGG2){:target="_blank"}

### Deep Work: Rules for Focused Success in a Distracted World, and So Good They Can't Ignore You: Why Skills Trump Passion in the Quest for Work You Love
- I read these back to back several times. I can't remember which ideas came from which book so I recommend them together. They have been valuable for steering me in the right direction to focus on skills that matter for a work life I can enjoy.
- [So Good They Can't Ignore You](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0076DDBJ6/ref=x_gr_w_glide_sin?caller=Goodreads&callerLink=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F13529074-so-good-they-can-t-ignore-you&tag=x_gr_w_glide_sin-20){:target="_blank"}
- [Deep Work](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00X47ZVXM/ref=x_gr_w_glide_sin?caller=Goodreads&callerLink=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F25744928-deep-work&tag=x_gr_w_glide_sin-20){:target="_blank"}
